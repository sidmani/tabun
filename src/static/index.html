TABUN

distributed spaced-repetition system

Tabun is a spaced-repetition flashcard application that runs in your browser.

It stores your data in open, human-readable formats, and uses versioning in shared flashcard decks to allow simple and transparent updates.

Tabun is open-source software licensed under the terms of the GNU-AGPLv3 and developed cooperatively on GitHub.
